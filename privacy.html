Privacy Policy

Welcome to Pro Gaming Zone. Your privacy is critically important to us. This document outlines the types of personal information received and collected by our platform and how it is used.

Log Files

Pro Gaming Zone follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. The information collected includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, referring/exit pages, and the number of clicks. This data is not linked to any personally identifiable information.

Google AdSense DoubleClick DART Cookies

Google is a third-party vendor on our site. It uses cookies, known as DART cookies, to serve targeted advertisements to our site visitors based upon their visit to our website and other pages across the internet.

Our Advertising Partners

Some of the advertisers on our site may use cookies and web beacons. Our primary advertising partner is Google AdSense. Third-party ad servers use technology to directly send the advertisements and links that appear on our platform to your browser.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or require more information about our Privacy Policy, please feel free to contact us.
